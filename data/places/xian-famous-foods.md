Category: Food
Neighborhood: Midtown
Address: 24 W 45th St, New York, NY 10036
Link: https://www.xianfoods.com
Google Maps: [View on Google Maps](https://maps.google.com/maps?q=24+W+45th+St+New+York+NY+10036)

From UN HQ: ~12 min walk west to 6th Ave then north

Why it is good:
- Hand-ripped noodles (biang biang style), spicy cumin lamb burgers, and cold skin noodles from the Xi'an region of China — bold flavors at unbeatable prices.
- Beloved NYC institution with multiple locations; always worth the short wait.

Dietary notes:
- Vegetarian and vegan options available (spicy cumin tofu, cold sesame noodles); spice level can be adjusted.

Tips:
- Counter service — order at the front, pick up when called.
- Seating is limited; best to take food to Dag Hammarskjöld Plaza or Bryant Park if weather allows.
- Budget around $12–15 per person all-in.
