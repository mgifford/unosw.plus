Category: Park
Neighborhood: Turtle Bay
Address: E 47th St between 1st Ave & 2nd Ave, New York, NY 10017
Google Maps: [View on Google Maps](https://maps.google.com/maps?q=Dag+Hammarskjold+Plaza+New+York+NY)

From UN HQ: ~5 min walk north on 1st Ave to 47th St

Why it is good:
- Named after the second UN Secretary-General — this plaza sits directly in front of the UN complex and is a natural gathering point for conference attendees.
- Benches, trees, a small fountain, and a Wednesday farmers market make it feel welcoming year-round.
- Perfect for spontaneous meetings between sessions; you will likely run into other OSW attendees here.

Dietary notes:
- Outdoor seating only; the Wednesday green market sells fresh food and snacks.

Tips:
- Green market runs every Wednesday, rain or shine (roughly 8 AM–3 PM).
- The UNICEF sculpture and garden on the northern side is a great backdrop for photos.
- Shaded in the afternoon — arrive early morning for sunny seating.
