Why it is good:
- Community-focused NYC coffee shop known for thoughtful sourcing and a cozy atmosphere.
- Often has board games on the tables — deliberately encourages people to slow down and connect.

Dietary notes:
- Oat and almond milk; gluten-free snacks usually available.

Tips:
- Best for a relaxed mid-afternoon meetup rather than a rushed morning coffee run.
- Quieter than street-level Midtown cafes; good acoustics for conversation.
