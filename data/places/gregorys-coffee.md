Category: Coffee
Neighborhood: Midtown East
Address: 873 3rd Ave, New York, NY 10022
Link: https://www.gregoryscoffee.com
Google Maps: [View on Google Maps](https://maps.google.com/maps?q=873+3rd+Ave+New+York+NY+10022)

From UN HQ: ~8 min walk (head west on 42nd to 3rd Ave, then north to 51st)

Why it is good:
- NYC-born specialty coffee chain with fast service and reliable espresso — opens early for pre-event coffee runs.
- Enough seating for a small sidebar or impromptu debrief; good WiFi.

Dietary notes:
- Oat, almond, and soy milk available; pastry selection includes gluten-free options.

Tips:
- Busy during the 8–9 AM rush — arrive before 8 or after 9:30.
- Power outlets along the window counter are great for a quick working coffee.
