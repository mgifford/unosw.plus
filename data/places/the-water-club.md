Category: Restaurant
Neighborhood: Murray Hill
Address: East River at 30th St, New York, NY 10016
Link: https://www.thewaterclub.com
Google Maps: [View on Google Maps](https://maps.google.com/maps?q=The+Water+Club+30th+St+New+York+NY)

From UN HQ: ~20 min walk south along the river, or short taxi/Uber

Why it is good:
- A unique NYC experience — a restaurant permanently moored as a barge on the East River, with sweeping views of the Manhattan skyline, the Queensborough Bridge, and the river.
- A memorable and impressive dinner venue for an international delegation or a special occasion during the week.

Dietary notes:
- Seafood-forward menu; vegetarian and dietary accommodations available with advance notice.

Tips:
- Reservations are essential — book well in advance for weeknight dinner during OSW.
- Take a taxi or Uber rather than walking at night; the riverside approach can be dark.
- Sunday brunch is legendary and slightly more relaxed than weeknight dinner service.
