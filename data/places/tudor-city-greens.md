Category: Park
Neighborhood: Tudor City
Address: Tudor City Place, New York, NY 10017
Google Maps: [View on Google Maps](https://maps.google.com/maps?q=Tudor+City+Greens+New+York+NY)

From UN HQ: ~5 min walk (west on 42nd St, then up the stairs on Tudor City Place)

Why it is good:
- Two small elevated parks, technically private but open to the public, perched above 42nd St with views of the Chrysler Building and the UN.
- Incredibly peaceful for their central location — a genuine hidden gem that most Midtown visitors miss.

Dietary notes:
- Outdoor seating only; bring your own food or grab something from a nearby deli.

Tips:
- Entrance via the stairways on 42nd St between 1st and 2nd Ave, or from Tudor City Place.
- The north park gets afternoon shade; the south park has more sun.
- A fantastic spot for a quiet phone call or a private chat between sessions.
