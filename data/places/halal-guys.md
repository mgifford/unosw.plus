Category: Quick Bites
Neighborhood: Midtown
Address: W 53rd St & 6th Ave, New York, NY 10019
Google Maps: [View on Google Maps](https://maps.google.com/maps?q=Halal+Guys+53rd+6th+Ave+New+York+NY)

From UN HQ: ~20 min walk west, or 1 stop on the subway (E/M to 53rd/Lex, then walk)

Why it is good:
- The original NYC halal cart that launched a thousand franchises — chicken and gyro over rice with the famous white sauce and hot sauce, at prices that have barely changed in decades.
- An iconic NYC culinary experience in itself; internationally known among visitors.

Dietary notes:
- Halal certified; no pork products; vegetarian falafel platter available.

Tips:
- This is the original outdoor street cart at 53rd & 6th (different from the brick-and-mortar restaurant nearby).
- Cash preferred at the cart; credit cards usually accepted at the restaurant.
- Open late — good for post-evening-event food.
