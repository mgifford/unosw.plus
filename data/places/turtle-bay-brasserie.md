Category: Bar
Neighborhood: Turtle Bay
Address: 987 2nd Ave, New York, NY 10022
Link: https://www.turtlebayny.com
Google Maps: [View on Google Maps](https://maps.google.com/maps?q=987+2nd+Ave+New+York+NY+10022)

From UN HQ: ~8 min walk north on 1st Ave, left on 52nd St, right on 2nd Ave

Why it is good:
- Neighborhood French-American brasserie and bar right in the Turtle Bay district — genuinely local, frequently visited by UN and NGO staff.
- Sidewalk seating in good weather makes it a natural spot for spontaneous after-work gatherings.

Dietary notes:
- Vegetarian and vegan menu items; gluten-free options available.

Tips:
- Happy hour weekdays 4–7 PM with discounted drinks.
- Can get lively (and louder) after 8 PM; arrive early for conversation-friendly seating.
- The neighborhood is safe and easy to reach from the UN at the end of the day.
