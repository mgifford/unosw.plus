Google Maps: [View on Google Maps](https://maps.google.com/maps?q=849+Lexington+Ave+New+York+NY+10065)

From UN HQ: ~12 min walk west and north

Why it is good:
- NYC-born soup and salad chain with a large rotating menu of creative soups and hearty grain bowls.
- Fast, filling, and reliable for a working conference lunch — multiple Midtown locations make it easy to find.

Dietary notes:
- Vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options are clearly labeled on the menu boards.

Tips:
- Get there before 12:30 to beat the lunch rush; service slows 12:30–1:30 PM.
- Good for feeding a diverse group quickly — the menu accommodates most dietary needs.
- Online ordering available for pickup.
