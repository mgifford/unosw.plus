Category: Park
Neighborhood: Turtle Bay
Address: 217 E 51st St, New York, NY 10022
Google Maps: [View on Google Maps](https://maps.google.com/maps?q=217+E+51st+St+New+York+NY+10022)

From UN HQ: ~12 min walk north and west

Why it is good:
- One of NYC's finest vest-pocket parks, featuring a dramatic 25-foot waterfall that drowns out city noise.
- Despite being tucked between skyscrapers, it feels remarkably private and calming — perfect for a conversation you don't want overheard.
- A seasonal snack bar operates on-site (spring–fall).

Dietary notes:
- Snack bar offers light refreshments seasonally; free to enter.

Tips:
- Open daily approximately 8 AM–7:45 PM (hours vary seasonally).
- The waterfall creates natural white noise — great for confidential conversations.
- Brings a welcome contrast to the glass-and-steel surroundings; worth the short detour.
