Category: Bar
Neighborhood: Grand Central
Address: 89 E 42nd St (inside Grand Central Terminal), New York, NY 10017
Google Maps: [View on Google Maps](https://maps.google.com/maps?q=89+E+42nd+St+New+York+NY+10017)

From UN HQ: ~10 min walk west on 42nd St

Why it is good:
- Classic pub inside the iconic Grand Central Terminal — a conversation-starter location in one of the world's most beautiful train stations.
- Serves craft beers, wines, and hearty food; great for meeting attendees arriving by rail from out of town.

Dietary notes:
- Standard pub menu with vegetarian options.

Tips:
- Very convenient for travellers — arriving or departing by Metro-North or subway.
- Gets busy around the evening rush (5–7 PM); arrive before 5 PM or after 7 PM for more relaxed seating.
- The Grand Central surroundings are spectacular — worth a slow walk through the main concourse.
