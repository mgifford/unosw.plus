Category: Bar
Neighborhood: Midtown East
Address: 11 E 36th St, New York, NY 10016
Link: https://www.gingerman-ny.com
Google Maps: [View on Google Maps](https://maps.google.com/maps?q=11+E+36th+St+New+York+NY+10016)

From UN HQ: ~10 min walk west and south on 36th St

Why it is good:
- Beer bar with 70+ taps covering craft, import, and cask ales from around the world — a genuinely international selection that suits an internationally-minded crowd.
- Large, comfortable booths make it easy to seat a group for a post-session debrief.

Dietary notes:
- Pub food menu with vegetarian options; wide gluten-free beer selection.

Tips:
- Happy hour Monday–Friday 3–6 PM — a great early finish to a conference day.
- Quieter and more conversation-friendly than the tourist bars around Times Square.
- No reservations needed for small groups; larger parties (8+) should call ahead.
