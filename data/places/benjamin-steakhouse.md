Category: Restaurant
Neighborhood: Midtown East
Address: 52 E 41st St, New York, NY 10017
Link: https://www.benjaminsteakhouse.com
Google Maps: [View on Google Maps](https://maps.google.com/maps?q=52+E+41st+St+New+York+NY+10017)

From UN HQ: ~8 min walk west and south on 41st St

Why it is good:
- Elegant USDA prime dry-aged steakhouse housed in the stunning Art Deco Chemists Club building.
- One of the better fine-dining options within easy walking distance of the UN — ideal for a celebratory dinner or a high-stakes evening meeting.

Dietary notes:
- Non-beef options available (seafood, chicken); dietary restrictions accommodated with advance notice.

Tips:
- Reservations strongly recommended, especially for dinner.
- Business casual dress appropriate; the room is impressive.
- Weekday lunch prix fixe offers excellent value for the quality.
