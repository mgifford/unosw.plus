Category: Food
Neighborhood: Grand Central
Address: 52 Vanderbilt Ave, New York, NY 10017
Link: https://www.ammosny.com
Google Maps: [View on Google Maps](https://maps.google.com/maps?q=52+Vanderbilt+Ave+New+York+NY+10017)

From UN HQ: ~10 min walk (west on 42nd St to Vanderbilt Ave)

Why it is good:
- Upscale Greek seafood restaurant serving fresh Mediterranean dishes steps from Grand Central.
- The weekday lunch prix fixe is impressive enough for a meaningful side meeting without breaking the budget.
- Elevated atmosphere that suits an international delegation without being stuffy.

Dietary notes:
- Seafood-focused menu; excellent vegetarian mezze options; gluten-free accommodations available on request.

Tips:
- Lunch prix fixe (weekdays) is the best value — two courses plus dessert for a set price.
- Dinner is noticeably pricier; book lunch if budget is a concern.
- Reservations recommended for groups of four or more, especially at peak lunch (12:30–1:30 PM).
