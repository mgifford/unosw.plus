Category: Park
Neighborhood: Turtle Bay
Address: East River Greenway at E 42nd St, New York, NY 10017
Google Maps: [View on Google Maps](https://maps.google.com/maps?q=East+River+Esplanade+New+York+NY+42nd+St)

From UN HQ: ~3 min walk east to the river

Why it is good:
- The closest outdoor escape from the UN complex — a paved waterfront path with benches overlooking the East River, Roosevelt Island, and the borough of Queens.
- Great for a brief walking meeting, a breath of fresh air between sessions, or a morning run.

Dietary notes:
- No food vendors on the path itself; nearest options are on 1st Ave.

Tips:
- Access via the pedestrian ramp at the bottom of 42nd St or 47th St.
- The path runs continuously from about 34th to 60th St — good for longer walks.
- Can be windy near the water; bring a layer in shoulder seasons.
