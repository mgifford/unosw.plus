Category: Quick Bites
Neighborhood: Turtle Bay
Address: 1st Ave & E 46th St, New York, NY 10017
Google Maps: [View on Google Maps](https://maps.google.com/maps?q=1st+Ave+%26+E+46th+St+New+York+NY+10017)

From UN HQ: ~5 min walk north on 1st Ave

Why it is good:
- Classic NYC street-cart halal food — chicken or gyro over rice, loaded with salad and white sauce, for under $10.
- As close to the UN as it gets for a quick cheap lunch between sessions.

Dietary notes:
- Halal certified; vegetarian rice and salad combo usually available on request.

Tips:
- Cart positions shift slightly; look along 1st Ave between 44th and 47th St.
- Cash only at street carts.
- Perfect for grabbing food and eating at Dag Hammarskjöld Plaza a block away.
